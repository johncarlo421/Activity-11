BACHELOR OF COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY

As technology becomes more advanced, computers and the ways we use them advance as well. With a bachelor's degree in computer science, computer information technology or computer engineering technology, you can get started learning about computers, their maintenance and how emerging technologies will change them. Online learning options are available for all of these degree programs.

Program Objectives:

The Program prepares learners with the practical skills in multimedia technology, web mobile development, and network systems administration, and supervisorial skills to manage as organization in these areas of competenties, engage in a startup business of purpose a teaching career.

Specific Objectives:

The learner should be able to:

  1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance od being an ethical computing professional when making work-related decisions.
  2. Develop products of processes by applying their knowledge in computer or network system, and development of productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development/service team with leadership roles when appropriate.
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
  5. Make positive contribution to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the Program.
  6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiental and field-based opportunities.

Job Opportunities:

  • Visual graphics designer
  • Photographer
  • Video Editor
  • Front-end web developer
  • Back-end web developer
  • Full-stack web developer
  • System/network administrator
  • System maintenance personnel
  • Teacher/trainor
